Trusted Resources

Free Help for Oʻahu Homeowners

These are organizations and agencies I trust and refer homeowners to. All are free or low-cost. None of them will pressure you.

Foreclosure & Mortgage Help

HUD-Approved Housing Counselors

Free or low-cost counseling from federally approved agencies. They can help you explore loan modifications, repayment plans, and foreclosure alternatives. This is the first call to make if you're behind on your mortgage.

Find a counselor: hud.gov/find/counseling · (800) 569-4287

Legal Aid Society of Hawaiʻi

Free civil legal services for qualifying low-income residents. Can assist with foreclosure defense, tenant rights, and other housing-related legal matters on Oʻahu.

legalaidhawaii.org · (808) 536-4302

NeighborWorks Hawaii

HUD-approved housing counseling agency offering free foreclosure prevention counseling, financial coaching, and homebuyer education on Oʻahu.

neighborworkhawaii.org · (808) 595-6080

Legal Assistance

Hawaiʻi State Bar — Lawyer Referral Service

Connects you with a licensed Hawaiʻi attorney for a reduced-fee initial consultation. A good first step if you need legal advice but aren't sure where to start.

hsba.org · (808) 537-1868

Volunteer Legal Services Hawaiʻi

Provides free civil legal services to low-income individuals on Oʻahu through volunteer attorneys. Handles a range of housing and civil matters.

vlsh.org · (808) 528-7046

Property Tax & Government Agencies

City & County of Honolulu — Real Property Tax Division

Official source for property tax information, exemptions, appeals, and payment options for Oʻahu properties. If you have a property tax lien or unpaid taxes, start here.

honolulu.gov/rpt · (808) 768-3799

Hawaiʻi Office of Consumer Protection

State agency that enforces consumer protection laws, including rules against foreclosure rescue fraud. If you believe you've been targeted by a scam, report it here.

cca.hawaii.gov/ocp · (808) 587-3222

Financial Counseling & Credit Help

National Foundation for Credit Counseling (NFCC)

Find a nonprofit credit counseling agency near you. NFCC members offer budget counseling, debt management plans, and housing counseling — often for free or low cost.

nfcc.org · (800) 388-2227

Aloha United Way — 211 Hawaiʻi

Dial 2-1-1 to be connected with local social services including housing assistance, utility help, and emergency resources across Oʻahu.

Call or text: 211 · alohaunitedway.org/211
